Trying to keep up with technological growth can be time consuming. But, sometimes we need to find a healthy balance. So, when I am not growing my knowledge I do try and fit some things I enjoy. Here are a few.








Gaming





Having something to decompress after a long stressful day is so important to having a good balance in life.

I have always found playing video games as a form of escape. No, not an escape from life but a way to escape the stresses life can bring.

At a young age I have always gravitated towards playing vidoe games in my free time. It has always given me a certain spark to learn more about technology

During adulthood I often found myself messing around with technology but never taking it and running with it as a career.

That is when I decided to start coding and have since fallen in love.








Sports







Growing up in South-east Louisian has definitely instilled a love for sports in me.

As a kid seeing my family excited and having a good time watching and playing sports just left a lasting feeling in my heart

Growing up I loved playing sports and the competition that comes with it.

I feel this has always given me a slight edge in a lot of things i take part in as an adult.

Not being afraid of a challenge and stepping up to the plate.

Having this hypothetical edge it gives me the drive to thrive in many different ways.
